Hello! My name is Xin Tong, and I am currently a Ph.D. student enrolled in the Fall 2020 semester in the Department of Physics at Zhejiang University (ZJU).

I am fortunate to be under the guidance of Prof. Daomu Zhao from the Department of Physics and the invaluable support from Prof. Renjun Xu from the Department of Computer Science at ZJU. I've embarked on an enriching journey at the intersection of optics and computer science.
